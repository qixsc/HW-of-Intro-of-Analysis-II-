    And we can find a $N' > N$ s.t. $\dfrac{\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{N} a_i - a}{N'} < \dfrac{\epsilon}{2}$.
    Thus, for any $n > N'$, \begin{align*}
        |\dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i }{n} - a| &\leq |\dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^{N} a_i - a}{n}| + |\dfrac{\sum_{i=N+1}^{n} a_i - a}{n - N'}|\\
